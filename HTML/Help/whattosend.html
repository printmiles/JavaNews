If you do have a problem and are not able to resolve it within your means then please get in touch with me. Email me at: alexander.j.harris at btinternet.com with the following information. Due to the amount of information that can be sent, please ensure that any attachments are zipped using WinZip or a similar application.

When reporting a problem, please ensure that you send the following:

  • A screenshot of the problem and any error code shown
  • All log files created by the application
  • Details of the database type and version being used eg. Microsoft Access 2007
  • Your contact details

Once your problem report has been sent it will be acknowledged and any further information may be requested.
